1 . <!DOCTYPE html>

You'll need this tage at the beginning of every HTMLdoucement you create. It ensures that a

browser knows that it's reading HTML, and that is expects HTML5, the lastest version.

Even though this isn't actually an HTML tage, it's still good one to know.

2. <html>

This is another tag that tells a browser that it's reading HTML. The <html> tag goes

straight after the DOCTYPE tag, and you close it with a </html> tag right at the end of your

file. Everything else in your document goes between these tags

3. <head>

The <head> tag starts the header section of your file. The stuff that goes in here doesn't

on your webpage. Instead, it contains metadata for search engines, and info for

browser.
